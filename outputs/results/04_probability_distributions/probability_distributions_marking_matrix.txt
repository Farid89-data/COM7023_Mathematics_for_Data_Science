STEP 3: EXPECTED VALUE AND VARIANCE

--- Expected Value (Mean) ---

  Formula: E[X] = Σ xᵢ × P(X = xᵢ)

  For discrete random variable X:

  Step-by-step calculation:

  E[Grade] = Σ (midpoint × probability)

  Grade Band    Midpoint    P(G=g)    Contribution
  -------------------------------------------------------

  80 - 90         85.0     0.10         8.50
  70 – 79         74.5     0.20        14.90
  60 - 69         64.5     0.25        16.12

  0 - 19           9.5     0.01         0.10

  Expected Grade (Mean): E[G] = 62.83

--- Variance ---

  Formula: Var(X) = E[X²] - (E[X])²
          = Σ xᵢ² × P(X = xᵢ) - μ²

  Calculation:
  E[G²] = Σ(midpoint² × probability) = 4227.96
  Var(G) = E[G²] - (E[G])² = 4227.96 - (62.83)²
  Var(G) = 4227.96 - 3946.98 = 280.98
  SD(G) = √Var(G) = √280.98 = 16.76

STEP 4: CONDITIONAL PROBABILITY
======================================================================

--- Conditional Probability Formula ---

  P(A|B) = P(A ∩ B) / P(B)

  Reads: Probability of A given B

  Event Probabilities:
  P(Pass) = P(Grade ≥ 40) = 0.92
  P(Distinction) = P(Grade ≥ 70) = 0.35
  P(Fail) = P(Grade < 40) = 0.08

  Conditional Probability:
  P(Distinction | Pass) = P(Distinction ∩ Pass) / P(Pass)
                        = P(Distinction) / P(Pass)
                        = 0.35 / 0.92
                        = 0.3804

  Interpretation: Given a student passes, there is a
  38.0% chance they achieve distinction.

STEP 6: BINOMIAL DISTRIBUTION EXAMPLE
======================================================================

--- Binomial Distribution ---

  P(X = k) = C(n,k) × p^k × (1-p)^(n-k)

  where C(n,k) = n! / (k! × (n-k)!)

  Used when:
  - Fixed number of trials (n)
  - Two possible outcomes (success/failure)
  - Constant probability of success (p)
  - Independent trials

  Example: Class of 10 students, P(pass) = 0.92

  What is P(exactly k students pass)?

  k    P(X = k)      Calculation
  ------------------------------------------------------------
  0    0.000000      C(10,0) × 0.92^0 × 0.08^10
  1    0.000000      C(10,1) × 0.92^1 × 0.08^9
  2    0.000000      C(10,2) × 0.92^2 × 0.08^8
  3    0.000002      C(10,3) × 0.92^3 × 0.08^7
  4    0.000039      C(10,4) × 0.92^4 × 0.08^6
  5    0.000544      C(10,5) × 0.92^5 × 0.08^5
  6    0.005216      C(10,6) × 0.92^6 × 0.08^4
  7    0.034274      C(10,7) × 0.92^7 × 0.08^3
  8    0.147807      C(10,8) × 0.92^8 × 0.08^2
  9    0.377729      C(10,9) × 0.92^9 × 0.08^1
  10   0.434388      C(10,10) × 0.92^10 × 0.08^0

  Expected number of passes: E[X] = n × p = 10 × 0.92 = 9.20
  Variance: Var(X) = n × p × (1-p) = 0.74
